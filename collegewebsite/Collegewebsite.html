About Us

Established in 1979, Dayananda Sagar College of Arts, Science and Commerce is one of the leading institutions in Bengaluru. We offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various fields, including Arts, Science, and Commerce. Our college is known for its commitment to academic excellence, research, and holistic development of students.

Dayananda Sagar College of Arts, Science and Commerce is a premier institution offering quality education in various streams. Our mission is to nurture talent, encourage research, and create future leaders.
